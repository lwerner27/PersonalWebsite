About Me

Hi, I'm Lucas! I'm a developer seeking to build high performance web apps. I love being on the bleeding of technology (see suggestion 1) and being challenged to learn new things.

After a year spent gaining expertise in JavaScript, HTML and CSS, I wanted a framework that would allow me to put all these pieces together and I enrolled in a full stack certification program at the University of Minnesota.

Since successfully completing the program in July I have continued to pursue additional skills and industry knowledge. I am currently enrolled in an online course for Google's Go programming language and I am also trying to round out my knowledge base by picking up Angular or taking the suggested courses for various Amazon Web Services (AWS).

I’m doing all of this because I am looking for a new challenge. I’m passionate about this work and would be excited for the opportunity to use my experiences and skills to help build something awesome.
